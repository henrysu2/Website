Pet Adoption App

Prompt: The goals of this project is to match every pet with an owner who'll be able to give it the longest, happiest living conditions, and life. This project will be completed with an end goal in mind of trying to match every pet with the right family. The actors who will be impacted by this project will be families associated with pets, and any domestically livable pet. This project can be completed better with the involvement of animal shelters and pet stores.
